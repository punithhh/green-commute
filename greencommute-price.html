❓ Pricing FAQ
💸
Does GreenCommute charge a fee?
Currently 0% commission during beta. Future: 8–10% platform fee on paid rides only.
🎁
Can I offer free rides?
Yes! Many drivers offer free rides for community goodwill. Free rides earn extra trust score points.
💳
How do I pay?
Cash directly to driver, or UPI/GPay (future feature). Always confirm payment method in chat.
🔄
Can price be negotiated?
Yes — use the in-app chat to discuss pricing with your driver before confirming.
